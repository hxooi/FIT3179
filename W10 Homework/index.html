Unicorn Startup Companies

A unicorn refers to a privately held startup company with a valuation exceeding US$1 billion. The term was introduced in 2013, drawing inspiration from the mythical creature to symbolize the rarity of such achievements in the business world. To attain unicorn status, companies must demonstrate exceptional innovation, creativity, and a clear vision for sustained future growth.

Unicorn Startup Companies around the World

Unicorns aren’t just found in one place — they’re all over the world! Today, there are more than 1,000 unicorn startups across 46 countries, covering five regions: the Americas, Asia, Europe, Africa, and Oceania. This shows how innovation is happening everywhere, not just in one corner of the globe.

Unicorn Startup by Industry

Unicorn startups come from over 15 industries, such as Edtech, Artificial Intelligence, and Mobile and Telecommunications. Most unicorns, however, are found in the Fintech and Internet Software and Services industries, showing where innovation is strongest today.
